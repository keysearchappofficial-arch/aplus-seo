Positioning

More Than a Converter, It Is a Bridge Node Inside the Signal Workflow

The value of U-Hub is not only in format conversion, but in building practical bridge paths between different devices, signal standards, and workflow structures.

When SDI, HDMI, and NDI workflows all exist in the same environment, the real question is not whether one cable can connect to another, but whether the whole signal path can enter, exit, remain synchronized, and preserve the expected image quality and latency performance.

What Makes It Different

How Is It Different from Standard Converters?

More Than One-Way Conversion

U-Hub supports multi-directional conversion between 12G-SDI, NDI HX, and HDMI, allowing it to function as an encoder, decoder, or intermediate bridge node depending on the workflow.

More Than Only Encoding or Only Decoding

With integrated encode-decode design, one device can take on different roles on site, improving deployment flexibility and reducing planning complexity.

More Than Just “Having a Picture”

Beyond conversion itself, U-Hub emphasizes 4Kp60, YCbCr 4:2:2, high-quality encoding and decoding, low latency, and audio-video synchronization, making it suitable for more formal production and display workflows.

More Than a Desktop Device, It Is a Deployable System Component

Compact and plug-and-play, it fits into existing systems, temporary projects, or gradually evolving IP-based infrastructures as a stable signal bridge component.

Common Pain Points

If You Are Facing These Situations, U-Hub Becomes More Valuable

Mixed Formats Make Signal Routing Hard to Organize

The source may be SDI, the display may be HDMI, and the transport may be expected to run over NDI. Without a bridge node, the overall system quickly becomes fragmented and difficult to maintain.

The Issue Is Not Whether It Converts, but Whether Latency and Sync Stay Under Control

The real difficulty of signal conversion is often not whether there is an image, but whether latency, audio-video sync, and image quality remain reliable enough for actual use.

You Want to Move Toward IP-Based Workflows, but Cannot Rebuild the Whole System at Once

When existing equipment is still based on SDI or HDMI, but new workflows want to adopt NDI, a practical bridge solution is often needed to support the transition.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is It an Encoder or a Decoder?

It can serve as both. U-Hub uses an integrated encode-decode design and can be used as an encoder, decoder, or bridge endpoint depending on the scenario.

Does It Support 4K?

Yes. Both encoding and decoding support up to 4Kp60 (UHD).

Is the Latency High?

U-Hub is designed for low-latency transmission. End-to-end latency can be kept below 100ms while maintaining good audio-video synchronization.

Is It Suitable for Conference Rooms or Business Applications?

Yes. In addition to production workflows, it is also suitable for conferencing input/output and other environments that need lower-latency display integration.

Is It Better for New System Builds or Existing System Transition?

Both. It works for new installations, and it is also highly suitable as a bridge node for gradually connecting existing SDI / HDMI systems into NDI-based workflows.
