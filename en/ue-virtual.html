Technical Strengths

Core Technical Capabilities

The value of these capabilities is not simply that there are many of them, but that they work together to support a more complete virtual production workflow.

PBR Real-Time Rendering

Uses physically based rendering to process light, color, materials, and environments in real time, supporting PBR materials, ray tracing, water systems, sky environments, ambient lighting, and particle effects.

Integrated Broadcast Control

Combines switching, graphics, packaging, camera control, and lighting control into one interface, reducing the collaboration cost of multiple systems.

Step-Based Timeline Workflow Control

Camera positions, graphics, packaging, camera movement, and switching points can be placed into one timeline logic, making the production process more orderly and easier to repeat.

3D Ray-Traced Keying

Through real-time 3D studio modeling and dynamic lighting capture, hair detail, shadows, and edges are preserved while color recovery and tonal integration are improved.

Intelligent Tracking and Stable Motion Control

Combines image recognition, pose estimation, and multiple tracking methods to strengthen virtual camera movement and overall image stability.

4K Virtual Camera Movement and Dual-Channel Output

Uses high-resolution signals to create more possibilities for virtual close-ups and wide shots, while also supporting the output and monitoring requirements of real production workflows.
